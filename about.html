About

The Beyond Journal is a podcast made by Mylea Hildebrand and Madison Posthumus, two university students based in Winnipeg, Manitoba.

It is set in an alternate version of 2020 where the pandemic didn't happen, because we said so.

Mylea Hildebrand

Voice of Mylea Smith

Mylea is the lead writer, and as you've probably figured out, lead actor. She is a first year university student at the University of Manitoba. Her hobbies include hanging out with her cats, reading, and contemplating existence.

Madison Posthumus

Madison does everything else- editing, production, distribution, and web design. In fact, I'm the one typing this blurb. Hello! I'm also a first year university student at the University of Manitoba. I'm currently in the U1 program, taking a majority of arts courses. My hobbies involve wasting my life online, binging podcasts, hanging out with cats, and existing in a constant state of chaos and confusion.
