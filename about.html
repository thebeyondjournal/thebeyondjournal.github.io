About

The Beyond Journal is a podcast made by Mylea Hildebrand and Madison Posthumus, two university students from Winnipeg, Manitoba.

Mylea Hildebrand

Voice of Mylea Smith

Mylea is the lead writer, and as you've probably figured out, lead actor. She is a second year actuarial sciences student at the University of Manitoba. Her character is highly based on herself, as she also wakes up at 5:30 and drinks oat milk. Her hobbies include hanging out with her cats, reading, and contemplating existence.

Madison's notes: I don't understand anything about her but I love her anyways
