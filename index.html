Welcome

The Beyond Journal is a fictional serial horror podcast which releases bi-weekly on Sundays. Follow Mylea Smith, a first-year university student, as she records a journal of her mundane difficulties of student life. As time passes, she begins to notice that some events have more to them than meets the eye, and finds herself immersed in a world of cults, secret agencies, and unknowable eldritch forces.
